About

I am a fan of minimalistic and aesthetic designs. I   am working hard to stay up to date with the most relevant and trendy design

I graduated from Belarusian National Technical University with a bachelor’s degree in Design of packaging. I have 5 years of experience in graphic design which helps me to understand colors, typography, composition and visual components better.

I have a specialized education in the program “UX/UI Design: Websites”. I have also completed an internship in UPROCK School under the mentoring of art director Evgeny Kuzmin.

My main aim is to improve my skills by reading articles, professional literature, watching tutorials, and then apply this knowledge to my work.

Workflow

01

01 / Briefing

Firstly, I ask to provide as much information about the project as possible. Goals, needs, details, and nuances
02

02 / Research

This step requires research about the industry, as well as competitive and visual analysis
03

03 / Prototyping

Based on the research and analysis, I develop a structure of the future project and the functionality of each page separately
04

04 / Design

I select references for creating unique and effective designs. I analyze various options for visual solutions and select fonts, colors, icons, and other graphic elements. I present 2-3 pages and after approval of the design I develop all other pages
05

05 / Adaptation

I draw adaptive versions for different electronic devices such as tablets and phones
06

06 / finalization

I prepare a Figma file with a fully developed project for transferring to a developer
